Our Story

Who we are and what we stand for

Company Overview

Kirtane & Pandit is a leading full-service chartered accountancy firm with over two decades of expertise in financial advisory, audit, and tax consultation. We serve corporations, SMEs, and high-net-worth individuals across India.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional financial advisory services that drive growth, ensure compliance, and build lasting value for our clients through integrity, expertise, and innovation.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Unwavering commitment to honesty and transparency
  • Excellence: Highest standards in all our work
  • Client Focus: Your success is our success
  • Innovation: Embracing modern solutions and practices
  • Teamwork: Collaborative approach to problem-solving

Unique Positioning

We combine traditional chartered accountancy expertise with modern financial technology, providing our clients with cutting-edge solutions backed by decades of industry experience. Our multi-city presence ensures local expertise with pan-India reach.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to half the height of the letter "K". Do not place other elements within this space.

Minimum Size

Digital: 120px width
Print: 1.5 inches width

Logo Don'ts

  • ❌ Do not alter the proportions or aspect ratio
  • ❌ Do not rotate, skew, or distort the logo
  • ❌ Do not add drop shadows or effects
  • ❌ Do not use unapproved color variations
  • ❌ Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • ❌ Do not combine with other logos without approval

Usage Guidelines

  • Primary: Navy Blue for main headers, backgrounds, and key elements
  • Accent: Gold for highlights, CTAs, and important information
  • Text: Charcoal (#333) for body text on light backgrounds
  • Backgrounds: White or Light Gray for content areas
  • Contrast: Maintain minimum 4.5:1 ratio for accessibility

Typography

Font families and typographic hierarchy

Font Families

Playfair Display (Serif)

Headings & Titles

Use for: H1, H2, H3 headings, logos, display text

System Font / Roboto (Sans-serif)

Body text, navigation, UI elements

Use for: Body copy, labels, descriptions

Typographic Hierarchy

H1 Heading — 48px Bold

Page titles and major sections

H2 Heading — 36px Bold

Section headers

H3 Heading — 28px Bold

Subsection headers

Body Text — 16px Regular

Primary content and paragraphs. Line height: 1.6

Small Text — 14px Regular

Captions, labels, metadata. Line height: 1.5

Usage Rules

  • Line Height: 1.6 for body text, 1.2 for headings
  • Letter Spacing: Default spacing, no additional spacing on headings
  • Font Weight: Regular (400) for body, Bold (700) for headings
  • All Caps: Reserve for labels and small-caps only, not body text
  • Links: Blue color with underline, change color on hover

Voice & Tone

How we communicate with our audience

Brand Personality

  • Professional: Formal, authoritative, competent
  • Trustworthy: Reliable, transparent, ethical
  • Forward-thinking: Modern, innovative, progressive
  • Client-centric: Attentive, empathetic, solution-focused

Writing Style

  • Use clear, jargon-free language when possible
  • Explain complex financial concepts simply
  • Active voice preferred over passive
  • Short sentences and paragraphs for scannability
  • Use numbers and bullet points effectively

Messaging Pillars

Expertise

We bring 20+ years of chartered accountancy experience and deep industry knowledge to every engagement.

Integrity

Honesty and transparency are at the core of everything we do. Your interests come first.

Innovation

We combine traditional expertise with modern technology to deliver cutting-edge solutions.

Partnership

We're not just advisors—we're your partners in growth, invested in your long-term success.

Tone Do's & Don'ts

✓ Do

  • Be professional and confident
  • Use "we" and "you" language
  • Be specific and data-driven
  • Show respect for client expertise
  • Use positive, solution-focused language

✗ Don't

  • Use overly casual or slang language
  • Make promises you can't keep
  • Use unnecessary technical jargon
  • Sound condescending or patronizing
  • Make claims without evidence
